ICMS 2026

Wilfrid Laurier University and University of Waterloo
Waterloo, Ontario, Canada, 20-23 July, 2026

The International Congress on Mathematical Software is a bi-annual congress that gathers the mathematicians, scientists and computer scientists who are interested in the development of mathematical software. ICMS 2026 is a satellite meeting of the International Congress of Mathematicians that is being held in Philadelphia the following week. For more information on past conferences, see the ICMS home page.

Participants are encouraged to check the Government of Canada entry requirements web site to determine whether a visa is necessary.
